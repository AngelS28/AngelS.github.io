Karina

Biography

Karina (카리나)/Yu Ji-min (유지민) is a South Korean singer and rapper under SM Entertainment. She is the leader of the girl group aespa.

Career

2019:Pre-debut
On February 2019, Karina appeared in Taemin's "Want" music video and also acted as backup dancer for the song's promotional stages.

2020: Debut with aespa
On October 28, 2020, Karina was revealed as the second member of aespa. The group debuted on November 17 with the digital single "Black Mamba". Before her debut with aespa, on November 1, she performed alongside Kai for the virtual showcase held by Hyundai Motors.

Personal Life

Education
Karina attended Hansol High School in South Korea before she took her GED to graduate.
