Aespa

Introduction

Aespa(에스파, also stylized as æspa) is a four member girl group under SM Entertainment. They debuted on Nov,17,2020 with the digitial single song called: "Black Mamba." Their name is a combination of "æ", which means 'Avatar X Experience', and "aspect", which means double-sided. Together it means that you would being able to experience a new world through another avatar of yourself.

History

Pre-debut activities
On September 19, 2016, Ningning was revealed as a new member of the trainee team SM ROOKIES. She appeared on the "Rookies Princess: Who's the Best?" segment of the program "My SMT" in 2016 and was also part of the soundtrack for the Korean animated TV program "Shining Star" in 2017. On February 2019, Karina appeared in Taemin's "Want" music video. She also performed with him on music shows in the following weeks.

2020: The Debut Year
On October 26, 2020, an intro video was released on aespa's social media announcing their name and logo. Later that day, it was announced that SM would be debuting a new girl group in November.The next day, the group's first member, Winter, was officially revealed. On October 28, the second member, Karina, was revealed.The same day, a video shown during a press conference featuring Lee Soo-Man revealed the group will have four members, including Giselle and Ningning. On October 29, Ningning was officially revealed as the group's third member. On October 30, Giselle was revealed as the group's fourth and final member. Incredible PC game bundle, from $10 BUY FROM FANATICAL On November 2, it was announced the group would debut on November 17 with the digital single "Black Mamba".The music video for the song counted 21,4225,062 views in its first day, breaking the record for most viewed music videos in the first 24 hours by a K-pop group in its debut, which was previously held by ITZY with the song "Dalla Dalla".
