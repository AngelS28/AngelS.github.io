Winter

Biography

Winter (윈터)/Kim Min-jeong (김민정) is a South Korean singer under SM Entertainment. She is a member of the girl group aespa.

Career

2020:Debut with Aespa
On October 27, 2020, Winter was revealed as the first member of aespa. The group debuted on November 17 with the digital single "Black Mamba". On December 6, she was among the artists who performed BoA's 20-year career tribute at the 2020 Mnet Asian Music Awards. She performed BoA's debut song, "ID; Peace B".
