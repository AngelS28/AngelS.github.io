NingNing

Biography

Ningning (Korean: 닝닝; Chinese: 宁宁)/Níng Yìzhuō (宁艺卓) is a Chinese singer under SM Entertainment. She is the maknae(the youngest) of the girl group aespa.

Career

2016-2017:SM ROOKIES
On September 19, 2016, Ningning was revealed as a new member of the trainee team SMROOKIES, alongside Yiyang. In September 2016, she was part of the "Who's the best?" segment of My SMT along with fellow rookies Koeun, Hina, and Yiyang. Later in 2017-2018, she along with Koeun and Hina sang several OST for the MBC animated series Shining Star.

2020:Debut with aespa
On October 29, 2020, Ningning was revealed as the third member of aespa. The group debuted on November 17 with the digital single "Black Mamba".
