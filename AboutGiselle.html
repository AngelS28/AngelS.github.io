Giselle

Biography

Giselle (Korean: 지젤; Japanese: ジゼル)/Uchinaga Eri (内永 枝利) is a Japanese-Korean rapper and singer under SM Entertainment. She is a member of the girl group aespa.

Career

2020:Debut with Aespa
On October 28, 2020, Giselle was revealed as a member of aespa through a video shown during a press conference with SM Entertainment founder Lee Soo Man. On October 30, she was officially revealed as the group's fourth and final member. The group debuted on November 17 with the digital single "Black Mamba".

Fun Fact

  • Her mother is Korean while her father is Japanese.
  • Upon her reveal, fans said she resembles f(x)'s Krystal and Girl's Generatioin's Yuri.
  • She is fluent in Korean, English, and Japanese.
  • She was a trainee for less than a year making her become SM Entertainment female idol with the shortest training period.
  • She was a fan of BLACKPINKand GOT7.
  • In the group, her representative symbol is crescent moon.
  • Her favorite color is black.
  • She used to be a representative athlete (High Jump) for her school in the 2017 Outdoor Season.
  • She personally wrote the lyrics for her SYNK teaser in three different languages (Korean, English, Japanese).
